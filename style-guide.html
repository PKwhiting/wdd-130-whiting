Style Guide

Purpose and Audience

Many federal credit unions find themselves in direct competition with dealerships in offering lending options and warranty/insurance packages for individuals. Many of these federal credit unions do much of this paperwork by hand and sometimes have a hard time understanding what all they should include in a package for an individual buying a car. From GAP insurance to roadside assistance, each vehicle, needs its own personalized package to best fit the owner and vehicle needs. This website will give us a central location for Credit Unions and other institutions who are interested in our software. They will be able to learn more about the software, connect with us to learn how it can help them, and to potentially buy the software.

Logo:

QuiKar

Use logo in black or tertiary color.

Typography:

Logo: Baloo Chettan 2, cursive

Headers: Convergence, sans-serif

Normal Text: Arvo, serif



Justification:

These font are soft gentle fonts that are inviting and are easily legible. They go along with the more laid back color choice and offer a more inviting experience.


Colors:

Primary Color:...........Headers/Text/Background/Links

Secondary Color:... white...Headers/Text/Cards/Links

Tertiary Color:............Link Hover/Logo/Buttons

Quaternary Color:....Accents

Quinary Color:...........Accents

Senary Color:..............Input Boxes/Buttons


Element Styling:

All paragraphs should have the opposing color(primary or secondary). Large paragraphs are placed on a white card with slight shadow except for the about us page. Each Page has a banner along the top stating the page. All input boxes will have a back color of the senary color. All pages have a header and footer with changing color links and Logo.
